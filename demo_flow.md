# Shuttle ETA — 2-3 Min Live Demo Script & Flow

This script is designed for a fast-paced, high-impact **2 to 3-minute live demo** to run immediately after your teammate finishes presenting the slides.

---

## 🛠️ Quick Pre-Demo Setup

1. **Get the URL:** Copy your live Vercel URL (e.g. `https://unilag-shuttle-eta.vercel.app`).
2. **Open Tab 1 (Projector view):** Load the homepage (`/`) on your laptop. Keep the **"Live Demo"** section visible.
3. **Open Tab 2 (Admin view):** Load the dashboard `/dashboard` page and click **"Continue as Guest (Anonymous)"** to sign in instantly.
4. **Reset:** In the bottom-right corner, open the **Presenter Console** pill and click **Reset Demo Environment** to clean baseline averages.

---

## 🎙️ Step-by-Step Presentation Script

### ⏱️ Minute 0:00 - 0:45 | Step 1: Scan & Frictionless Access
* **Action:** Keep **Tab 1** (Homepage) projected. Ask the judges to scan the dynamic QR code on the screen.
* **Script:**
  > *"To show you how frictionless this is, let's look at the live demo. There is no app store redirection, no download wait, and no sign-up forms required. Judges, please pull out your phones and scan the QR code on the screen right now.*
  >
  > *Once scanned, your phone immediately opens the rider view. It shows you're at the 'Faculty' stop, and calculated the ETA for the next shuttle is 7 minutes."*

---

### ⏱️ Minute 0:45 - 1:30 | Step 2: The Core Loop (Instant Sync)
* **Action:** Ask one of the judges to tap **"I'm on Shuttle A"** on their phone.
* **Script:**
  > *"Now, when the shuttle pulls up, all the student has to do is tap one single button on their phone: 'I'm on Shuttle A'. Go ahead and tap it on your phone now.*
  >
  > *Boom! Notice the screen instantly updates. No expensive GPS hardware on the buses, no complex trackers. The students themselves are the tracking network. Within seconds, the ETA gets recalculated for every student on campus."*

---

### ⏱️ Minute 1:30 - 2:15 | Step 3: PESSA Admin Operations
* **Action:** Switch to **Tab 2** (Dashboard) on your laptop.
* **Script:**
  > *"This data flows directly to university operators. In our live console, PESSA administrators can see active shuttles, track riders, and monitor wait metrics. We have a live route map showing where the shuttles are, a peak-hour heatmap, and a real-time feed.*
  >
  > *(Click 'Simulate Rush Hour' on the bottom-right Presenter Console)*
  >
  > *If we simulate rush hour, the dashboard instantly maps the surge, helping school admin optimize shuttle routing on the fly."*

---

### ⏱️ Minute 2:15 - 2:30 | Wrap-up
* **Script:**
  > *"We aren't asking for more buses—we're asking for the information students already deserve. Stop guessing, start knowing. Thank you."*

---

## 🚨 Troubleshooting (If Wi-Fi fails)
* **If judges can't connect/scan:**
  * Open two tabs side-by-side on your presentation laptop: one at `/` and one at `/ride?stop=Faculty`.
  * Click **"I'm on Shuttle A"** on the right side and watch the left side update. Explain that this represents what the judge would see on their phone.
